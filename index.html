About Flamingo Flyers

Your Source for VR Entertainment

Welcome to flamingo Flyers! Crash down from a roller coaster into a mysterious cabin, and choose from a variety of game modes found within! 

Go skiing down a huge mountain, and explore deep caves full of waterfalls and glittering crystals! 

Survive the forest with nothing but a flashlight in the horror mode! Invite some friends and get scared together! 

Take a break for a while! Relax and socialise in the main area, or head to the gun range, and empty a clip into some dummies (or into your friends)

Or maybe try something closer to home, and play tag! You could even build your home with the sandbox building tools!

Come in and join the fun!
